Defining Happiness

The term “happiness” can be defined in many different ways, including:

  • Love/connection
  • Joy
  • Authentic pride
  • Hubristic pride
  • Harmony
  • Abundance
  • Serenity
  • Gratitude
  • Interest
  • Awe

Defining happiness for ourselves helps us to recognize what makes us happy and to pursue it actively.

Instructions:

Consider each of the above points and ask yourself which ones are you most drawn to. Then form your own definition of happiness. It can be a combination of two or more emotional experiences or an entirely different type of feeling.

It is quite likely that you are going to revise and refine your definition of happiness with time. Feel free to come back here as often as you want.
