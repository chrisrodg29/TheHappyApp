Making a Happy List

Instructions:

One sure-fire way to make yourself happier is to do things you love, and to do them often. Make a list of things/situations/activities that lead you to the feeling of happiness in the way that you have defined it.

  • Start with 2-3 things, then add to the list as you discover more and more happy triggers for yourself.
  • Trigger these situations often. Make habits of visiting places and creating scenarios where you find yourself feeling happy.
